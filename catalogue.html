FENYR SUPERSPORT is the latest HyperCar launched by us, a limited edition with a production run of only 100 cars in addition to 10 “Fenyr SuperSport Launch Editions”.

The Fenyr SuperSport’s exterior body is crafted in carbon fiber with extensive use of graphene composite. The custom-made RUF engine was specially developed for us.

$3M

DIVO is the most agile and dynamic car we has ever created - a perfect homage for the 110th anniversary, which will be held in 2019. A monumental driving machine, production will be strictly limited to just 40 units.

The DIVO is a more extreme. It represents a new interpretation of our philosophy ‘form follows performance’ with an uncompromising pursuit of the utmost aerodynamic efficiency. To shape the future, we have embraced the past.

$3.5M

LYKAN HYPERSPORT created by us is the Lykan HyperSport, which catapulted the company to international stardom when it took center stage as hero car in Universal’s Studios Fast & Furious 7.

With only 7 cars in existence, the Lykan HyperSport is a handcrafted masterpiece sporting a full carbon fiber body, a patented reverse dihedral door system, a jewel-encrusted key with diamond-lined headlights and an innovative holographic interactive display.

$3.4M

AGERA means “to take action” in Swedish. It is also short for the ancient Greek word Ageratos which means “ageless”. These are both suitable descriptors for this incredible and beautiful machine.

The Agera Became the 2010 Top Gear Hypercar of the Year. Agera featured a unique 7 speed transmission with a E-diff and wet clutch functionality developed by us, a transmission that truly enhanced performance and handling.

$2.5M

JESKO – the all-new megacar from Koenigsegg – is powered by a newly designed, 1280hp (1600hp on E85), twin-turbocharged V8 engine that benefits from significant changes to the Agera engine it replaces.

At the core of the newly designed engine is the world’s lightest V8 crankshaft. Weighing just 12.5 kilos, our new flat-plane 180-degree crankshaft allows Jesko to produce more power, with greater efficiency, while achieving a higher 8500rpm rev limit.

$2.8M

CHIRON is the fastest, most powerful, and exclusive production super sports car in our history. Its sophisticated design and performance make it a unique masterpiece of art, form and technique, that pushes boundaries beyond imagination.

We owe its distinctive character to a family of artists and engineers, and has always strived to offer the extraordinary.The result is a unique creation of enduring value, and breathtaking automotive accomplishment.

$2.9M
